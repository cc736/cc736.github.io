Spotify Concept

For Intro to Digital Product Design (INFO 1998), user research showed me that there is a disconnect between finding music and our friends on Spotify. My solution to this problem was an in-app sharing experience that doubles as a way to keep users within the product. Through this I faced two challenges: (1) finding a salient entry point and (2) folding this feature into Spotify's current flows.
